# Privacy-Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile and desktop applications
developed and published by Burak Güldali on the App Store, Google Play,
and Microsoft Store (collectively referred to as "our Apps" or "the App").

By downloading or using any of our Apps, you agree to the collection
and use of information as described in this policy.

────────────────────────────────────────
 INFORMATION WE COLLECT
────────────────────────────────────────

Not all Apps collect all types of data. Each App only collects what
is necessary for its functionality.

a) Account Information
   - Email address, display name (via Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In)
   - Only collected in Apps that require user authentication

b) Usage Data
   - App features used, session duration, in-app interactions
   - Collected anonymously to improve performance and user experience

c) Purchase & Subscription Data
   - Subscription status and purchase history (via RevenueCat)
   - We do NOT store payment card or billing details at any time

d) Advertisement Data
   - Anonymized ad interaction data (via Google AdMob)
   - Only in Apps that include a free ad-supported tier

e) Device & Sensor Data
   - Device model, OS version
   - Sensor readings (accelerometer, gyroscope, microphone, etc.)
     only where the App's core functionality requires it
   - Sensor data is processed locally on your device and is never
     uploaded to our servers

f) User-Generated Content
   - Text, images, audio, or other content you create within the App
   - Stored locally on your device or in your personal cloud account
     depending on the App

g) Location Data
   - Only collected if explicitly required by the App's features
   - Always requested with your permission

────────────────────────────────────────
 HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
────────────────────────────────────────

We use collected information to:
  - Provide, maintain, and improve App functionality
  - Process and manage subscriptions and in-app purchases
  - Deliver personalized content where applicable
  - Show relevant advertisements in ad-supported Apps
  - Respond to user support requests
  - Comply with legal obligations

 DATA STORAGE & RETENTION
────────────────────────────────────────

  - Cloud data is stored on secure servers provided by Firebase

8. COOKIES & TRACKING
────────────────────────────────────────

Our Apps do not use browser cookies. Analytics and advertising
